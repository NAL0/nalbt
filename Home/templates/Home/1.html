问候（我的）兄弟姐妹

“因为其他人，一个人就是一个人”。
这是一个概述Ubuntu重要方面的谚语。
它提醒我们人性的源泉。
Ubuntu哲学提醒我们，生命是无价的
并且社区比个人更重要，
因此，个人必须为了社区的利益而牺牲。
Ubuntu提醒我们要把自己看作别人并善待他人
尊重，这是我们人类的源泉。

今天，我们用许多名字称呼自己。我们中的一些人认为是非洲人，
一些美国人，一些亚洲人，另一些欧洲人，一些白人，一些黄人，
有些红色，有些是棕色，有些是中国人，有些是津巴布韦人，有些是南非人。

非洲人，他强调这些边界的人为性。
他是对的，这些边界确实是人为的。我想强调同样的事实
你和我有黑与白，Afrikan和美国人（南非人或中国人）的边界。
这些边界确实是人为的。
我想提醒我们，我们都有共同的祖先，共同的遗产。
有些人可能不同意我的观点，但我们都来自一个人类摇篮非洲的地方。
我们中的一些人可能否认它，但我们是家人。
在说这个回声曼德拉的彩虹之梦的梦想。
在说这个时，我强调了伟大的Lucky Dube的话，当他说“不同的颜色，一个人”。

Greetings (my) brothers and sisters

"A person is a person because of other people".
This is a proverb outlining a vital aspect of Ubuntu.
It reminds us of the source of our humanity.
Ubuntu philosophy reminds us that life is priceless
and that the community is more important than the individual,
thus the individual must sacrifice for the good of the community.
Ubuntu reminds us to see ourselves in others and to treat others
with respect, this is the source our humanity.

Today, we call ourselfs by many names. Some of us identify as Afrikans,
some Americans, some Asians, others Europeans, some white, some yellow,
some red, others brown, others Coloreds some Chinese, Some Zimbabweans, others South Afrikans.

Prof Patrick Otiene Lumumba reminds us (not in so many words) that the
europeans met up in berlin in 1884 and drew up the current political map of
Afrika, he stressed the artificiality of these borders.
He is right, these borders are indeed artificial. I want to stress the same fact
with the border between black and white, Afrikan and American, (South Afrikan or Chinese,) you and me.
These borders are indeed artificial.
I want to remind us that we all have common ancestory, a shared heritge.
Some may disagree with me but we all come from one place which is Afrika, the cradle of humankind.
Some of us may deny it but we are family.
In saying this echoe Mandela's Dream of a rainbow nation.
In saying this I reinforce the words of the great Lucky Dube when he said "Different colors, one people."

(My) brothers and sisters. The sky is our roof, the ground our floor and tha earth our home.
Afrikan proverb says "He who earns calamity, eats it with his family".
In the face of climate change this proverb is very suitabe, with disaster like cyclone idhai
increasing in frequency and severity. Deserts spreading and decreasing arable land.
It is obvious the big bad wolf is at our door.
Afrikan proverb says "Division is weakness, Unity is strength".
As the young people of the world we are the future governers and policy leader,
the future leaders of industry and media, the future military generals and technologists.
We must unite with a zero tolerance policy against climate change or be devoured by the big bad wolf.

Afrikan proverb tells us "To go fast go alone, to go far go together."
(My) brothers and sisters. Let us walk together to:
the end of hunger,
the end of poverty,
good health and wellbeing for all,
quality education for all,
gender equality,
clean water and sanitation for all,
affordable and clean energy for all,
decent work and inclusive economic growth,
industry, innovation and infrastructure,
reduced inequality,
sustainable cities and communities,
responsible consumption and production,
climate action,
life on water,
life on land,
peace justice and strong institutions,
global partnerships and co-operation.
