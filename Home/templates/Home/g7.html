The world is changing and Afrika is on the rise. Young Afrikans have come together to start up the Leseli Initiative which plans to upgrade Afrika's individual national grids into a fully intergrated smart grid running from Cape to Cairo and from Madagascar to Morroco. We are dedicated to the elimination of the use of fossil fuels and the complete adoption of renewable energy by 2063 on our continent, the Leseli Initiative is our programme aimed at achieving this goal.
